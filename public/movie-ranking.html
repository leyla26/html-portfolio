The Best Movies According To Leyla

My top 6 movies of all-time.

Midnight in Paris

The film actually tells the story of two people who have never truly loved each other, trying to maintain their relationship out of habit, while the leading man's journey of knowing and finding himself.

Every Child is Special

An emotional feature-length film about the education system in India and how each child is gifted with special talents.

The Colour of God

This film, set in Iran, tells the story of a visually impaired child's passion for constantly searching for God through dozens of painful experiences.

Pride and Prejudice

It is a beautiful period film that shows us how logic and pride can get in the way of a strong emotion like love and how love can be realized as an act of will.

City of Stars

A musical, romantic film.It beautifully tells how two lovers who are by each other's side during difficult times naturally separate from each other in their lives.

Eternal Sunshine Of The Spotless Mind

No matter how much we want to forget or erase our memories, they are the realities that make us who we are and that we never want to forget or erase.
